About Our School

Welcome to Greenvalley Matriculation High School, where we are committed to providing quality education and fostering a nurturing environment for our students. Established in 1990, our school has a proud tradition of academic excellence and holistic development.

Our Mission

To empower students to become lifelong learners, responsible citizens, and compassionate individuals through innovative teaching and a supportive community.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for inspiring excellence and shaping future leaders.
